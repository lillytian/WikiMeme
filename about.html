About Us

This website was created by a team of UT Austin computer scientists at the annual HackTX event held at the AT&T Conference Center. With little knowledge of high-level computer science and a hundred-dollar voucher for a website domain, the team "And I O.O.P." decided to make a website about what they know best: memes. In the span of twenty-four hours, the team was able to complete this dank website for all meme-lovers to enjoy. So please, search for your favorite memes and add missing ones to the database. And most importantly, don't forget to like, comment, and subscribe!
